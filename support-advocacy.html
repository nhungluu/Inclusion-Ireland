Advocacy Supports



National Advocacy Service


The National Advocacy Service provides and independent and free advocacy service for people with disabilities.

The National Advocacy Service offers a service to people with disabilities who

  • have limited supports or
  • need support with communication or
  • live in residential services or attend day services.

The National Advocacy Service telephone number is 0761 07 3000

The National Advocacy Service email address is info@advocacy.ie

You can write a letter to the National Advocacy Service to ask for support.

Their address is -
